About CMFC

The Perspective

CMFC offers a new approach to putting your idea's into concrete detail. No more buying pencils and paper or driving miles to meet with fellow writers. Stay at your place, coffee by your side, and write music together with your friends with our quick and easy-to-use music editing software. We aim to make it as easy as possible for musicians of all backgrounds to come to a common place to learn, compose, and share their creations with the world.
