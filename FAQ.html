General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at CMFC is only required if you will be accessing premium content. This will ensure the user has the quality items purchased.

Can I submit my own music templates or themes?

Currently, CMFC provides the only usable templates

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All prices for themes, templates and other items, including each seller's or buyer's account balance are in USD
Sellers

Who cen sell items?

Any registed user, who presents a work, which is genuine and appealing, can post it on CMFC.

I want to upload my music - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account
  • Go to the Upload section and upload your music
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about 24-48 hours.

What do I get from uploading music?

Here, at CMFC, we offer a platform of collaborative music composition with fellow artist

Why upload music here?

There are a number of reasons why you should join us:
  • A creative source of music composition .
  • Fast response/approval times.
  • We are not an exclusive marketplace. This means that you can upload your items on CMFC, as well as on any other marketplate, and thus increasing the artist interested in your artistry.

What are the payment options?

We accept Credit Cards, Paypal, and BitCoin ;)

When will I know who saw my project?

Whenever your project is seen, you will be notified and given the viewers profile.
Buyers

I want to see other artist projects - what are the steps?

Click the Artist tab, and select the project you want to see.
Once you select the projects, you can follow or contact the artist.

Is this the latest version of an item

Each item in CMFC is maintained to its latest version. It is also updated daily.
